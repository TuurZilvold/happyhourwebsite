In this day and age the Internet is one of the most important tools we use everyday. Thousands of terabytes of information get uploaded, shared and deleted on the internet everyday and it is now our main source of information. There is a lot of usefull and informative information on the internet, but there is also a lot of misleading information. This website may help you to judge wether information you see online is true and based on evidence or that it is fake and made to mislead you. There are three articles on the website which are written like conspiracies and misleading articles so that you can see what conspiracies and other misleading articles generaly look like on the internet. On this website you can also find tips on how to easily recognize misleading articles and conspiracies and those tips may also help you to be more critical about information you get of the internet. You are not obligated to believe what we say or do what we advise you to do when it comes to online information and if you do not agree with our statements on online information you have the right to think that.
