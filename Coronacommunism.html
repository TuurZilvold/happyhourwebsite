This is a red flag because you can't check this information, so this could all be made up. Secondly some things in this article are verifiable untrue. For example the text in the second image doesn't say "join forces to prevent covid-19" but it just says "stay at home, wear a mask and wash your hands to stop the spread of covid-19". Thirdly, some statements in this article don’t explain anything, such as the second part of the answer of the question of how covid-19 is used to achieve world domination. Lastly, there is nothing known about the author and you can't check if he has experience on the subject, he/she is writing about. So always make sure that the information that is given in the article is legit by checking with other sources and make sure that the author is experienced and has written other articles on the subject.
