Tips

These are some tips on how to spot misleading articles and conspiracies:

  1. Look at the source:
    Look at the source of the article and see if it has a good reputation.
  2. Be critical towards the article:
    Always be critical and ask yourself questions such as: With what intention was this article written? Is it too good to be true?.
  3. Inspect the evidence:
    Does the evidence definitely prove something and has the evidence not been selected or corrupted to support a particular statement?
  4. Look at the author:
    Check if the author is experienced on the subject he's writting and if he has written more articles about that subject.
  5. Do an online check on the article:
    Search the article with the word hoax or myth to see if other people have confirmed or disproved the article.
